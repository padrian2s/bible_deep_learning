16.2.7 Converting Between Directed and Undirected

Orice model directed poate fi convertit in unul undirected prin procesul de moralization: adaugam muchii intre toti parintii fiecarui nod ("casatorim" parintii) si apoi eliminam directiile. Conversia inversa nu este intotdeauna posibila - unele independente nu pot fi reprezentate directed.
